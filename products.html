Core Supply Categories

Common facility supply categories we can support via sourcing, distribution partners, and contract-aligned purchasing (availability varies by location and contract scope):

  • Janitorial & custodial consumables (planned/sourcing)
  • Restroom hygiene consumables (planned/sourcing)
  • Cleaning chemicals & disinfectants (planned/sourcing)
  • Paper products (towels, tissue, dispensers — planned/sourcing)
  • Trash liners, bins, and waste handling supplies (planned/sourcing)
  • Floor care supplies (mops, pads, microfiber, etc. — planned/sourcing)
  • Safety & PPE items (gloves, masks, signage — planned/sourcing)
Note: We list “planned/sourcing” categories to reflect procurement support without implying on-hand inventory.

Dispensers & Facility Fixtures

For restrooms and high-traffic facilities, we can include supply + install coordination as part of a scope of work:

  • Paper towel, tissue, and soap dispensers (planned/sourcing)
  • Hand sanitizer stations (planned/sourcing)
  • Odor control and restroom accessories (planned/sourcing)
  • Refill programs for multi-site facilities (planned/sourcing)
  • Delivery scheduling aligned to contract requirements
If your solicitation requires specific brands, SKUs, or “or-equal” substitutions, include them in the RFQ.

How to Request Products

Use our RFQ form and select “Supplies / Products” as part of your request. Include:

  • Facility type + location(s)
  • Item list (brand/SKU if required) or “or-equal” language
  • Quantities + delivery schedule
  • Compliance requirements (MSDS/SDS, green products, etc.)
  • Purchase method (one-time vs recurring)
